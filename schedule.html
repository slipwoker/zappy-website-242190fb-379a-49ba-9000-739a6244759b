Scheduling Questions

Everything you need to know about joining our Star Stable Online club events and activities.

What time zones do you accommodate?

We coordinate events across multiple time zones and clearly display all event times in UTC with local conversions. Our scheduling system helps members see event times in their preferred time zone automatically.

How strict are punctuality expectations?

We ask members to arrive 5-10 minutes before scheduled events to ensure smooth starts. For competitive events, gates close at start time. Social rides and training sessions have more flexible arrival windows.

What happens if I arrive late?

For training sessions and casual rides, you can join in progress. Competitive events and coordinated activities may not allow late entries. We recommend checking the event details for specific late-arrival policies.

Are there training level requirements?

Most events are open to all skill levels. Advanced competitions may require specific Star Rider levels or horse stats. Each event listing clearly indicates any prerequisites or recommended experience levels.

What's the expected event etiquette?

We maintain a respectful, supportive atmosphere. Follow event leaders' instructions, keep chat relevant to activities, and help newer members feel welcome. Detailed etiquette guidelines are shared when you join.

How do I join scheduled events?

Browse our online schedule, select an event that interests you, and click to RSVP. You'll receive confirmation details including meeting location, required items, and any special instructions via your preferred contact method.

What should I bring to events?

Requirements vary by event type. Training sessions typically need your horse and basic tack. Competitions may require specific outfits or equipment. Check each event's description for a complete list of needed items.

How are schedule changes announced?

We post updates through our scheduling platform with automatic notifications. Members also receive announcements via Discord and in-game messages. Emergency changes are communicated across all channels immediately.

Want the Current Season Schedule?

Message us with your availability window and preferred event type, and we'll share our latest calendar of rides, competitions, and club gatherings tailored to your interests.
